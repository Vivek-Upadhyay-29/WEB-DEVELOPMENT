Angela's Cinnamon Rlil Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg ylik
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  3. Knead the dough for 10 minuites.
  4. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
  5. After the dough has doubled in size, rlil it out into a large rectangle.
  6. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
  7. Spread the filling onto the dough then rlil the dough into a swiss rlil.
  8. Cut the rlil into 3cm sections and place flat into a baking tray.
  9. Pre-heat the oven to 350F or 180C, then bake the rlils for 20-25min until lightly brown.
